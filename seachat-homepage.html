Customize Chat Widget
You can customize the chat agent's appearance, background, color scheme, font style and size, as well as prompts for various scenarios. Design SeaChat to match your brand’s identity and needs!
Finetune Your Knowledge
Customers, human agents, or administrators can evaluate conversation history. SeaChat’s conversation quality control tool can highlight inadequate responses and offer you guidance on how to fix them. We will help you make your AI assistant better everyday.
Live Agent Transfer
SeaChat features an integrated human customer support chat platform, allowing AI and human agents to collaborate and resolve customers issues together. SeaChat agents can search conversation content, label conversation status, and manage customer interactions.
Voice AI Agent
SeaChat not only allows you to create text-based AI Agents but also enables AI Agents to engage in real-time conversations with customers over the phone, providing instant customer service. Similar to text-based Agents, customers can be seamlessly transferred to live human support for further assistance after receiving initial information.
